# SignThought 用「先想后看」取代逐词映射：手语翻译首次引入显式推理层

## 摘要

**手语翻译从模式匹配升级为语义推理，多模态 Agent 的感知范式正在被重写。**
- 港理工+川大团队提出 SignThought，用「隐式思维」层先组织语义，再回视频验证，替代传统逐词 gloss 映射
- Plan-then-ground 解码策略：先决定「说什么」，再从视频找证据——这是推理链在非文本模态的首次工程化落地
- 无 gloss 方法一致性显著提升，意味着多模态 Agent 的「理解-生成」解耦路径已被验证

锐评：手语翻译是个极窄场景，但「先推理后锚定」的范式迁移对所有涉及视觉-语言对齐的 Agent 都有参考价值——别只盯着翻译，看架构。

## 原文

What if sign language translation didn’t just match signs to words, but actually thought through meaning first?

Researchers from Hong Kong Polytechnic University and Sichuan University introduce SignThought. 

They replace the old word-by-word mapping with an explicit layer of “latent thoughts” that gradually organize meaning from video. Their plan-then-ground decoding first decides what to say, then checks the video for evidence.

The result: consistent gains over existing gloss-free methods across multiple benchmarks, with better coherence and faithfulness. Code and data are open-source.

Think in Latent Thoughts: A New Paradigm for Gloss-Free Sign Language Translation
