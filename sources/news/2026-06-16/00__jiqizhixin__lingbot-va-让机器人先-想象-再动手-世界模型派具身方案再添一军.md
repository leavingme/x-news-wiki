# LingBot-VA 让机器人先"想象"再动手，世界模型派具身方案再添一军

## 摘要

**把视频预测塞进机器人控制回路，长程操作终于开始有"前瞻"能力。**
- LingBot-VA 用共享潜空间把视频预测与动作决策融合，并加闭环校正
- 长程操控任务上 SOTA，微调数据更少，且能泛化到新场景
- 代码已开源，定位是具身智能"决策-感知-想象"一体化

V-JEPA 路线的工业落地版本。当机器人开始"想象未来再行动"，纯反应式控制的天花板就被抬起来了。

## 原文

What if robots could imagine the future to decide their next move?

Enter LingBot-VA, a new AI framework that blends video prediction with robot control. 

It learns to “imagine” how actions change the world, using a shared space for vision and action, plus a loop that constantly checks against real observations. 

The result? Outperforms existing methods in long-horizon manipulation, needs less data for fine-tuning, and generalizes to new setups. Code is now public.
