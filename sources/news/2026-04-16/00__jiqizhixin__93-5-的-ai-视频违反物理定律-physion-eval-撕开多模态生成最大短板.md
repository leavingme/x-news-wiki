# 93.5% 的 AI 视频违反物理定律：Physion-Eval 撕开多模态生成最大短板

## 摘要

**[AI 视频物理真实性基准出炉，顶尖模型全线溃败]**

- Stanford、MIT、Harvard、Character AI 联合发布 Physion-Eval，用专家人类推理诊断 AI 视频物理违规：83.3% 外视角、93.5% 内视角视频至少一次违反现实物理
- 研究揭示当前 SOTA 模型在物理常识上的系统性失败，且"哪里出错"可以精确诊断
- 对 AI 视频工业化应用（影视、工业仿真、具身训练数据）是严重警示，也指明了下一代多模态模型必须攻克的方向

gakki：90% 以上的失效率不是"还有 10% 可用"，而是"这 10% 的边界目前完全不清楚"——在物理仿真要求高的场景，AI 视频仍是高风险素材。

## 原文

How physically realistic are our AI-generated videos? 

Physion Labs, Stanford University, MIT, Harvard University, and Character AI introduce Physion-Eval. 

This new benchmark uses expert human reasoning to meticulously diagnose and explain physical realism failures in AI-generated videos, pinpointing exactly why and where models violate real-world physics. 

Their findings are striking: 83.3% of exocentric and 93.5% of egocentric videos from leading AI models exhibit at least one human-identifiable physical glitch, revealing a critical gap in AI's ability to simulate the physical world.

Physion-Eval: Evaluating Physical Realism in Generated Video via Human Reasoning
