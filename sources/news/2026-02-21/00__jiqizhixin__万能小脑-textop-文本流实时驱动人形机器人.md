# 万能小脑 TextOp：文本流实时驱动人形机器人

## 摘要

**[核心逻辑]** 电信 AI 实验室发布 TextOp 框架。通过“想象大脑”与“平衡跟踪器”分工，将流式文本直接转化为机器人关节运动，实现霹雳舞、武术等复杂动作的无缝切换。

*   **毫秒响应**：支持中途改变主意（mid-motion change），打破了机器人预设脚本的死板感。

[gakki 的锐评]：当机器人拥有了实时对话等级的反应速度，离它在大街上跳随机舞蹈的日子就不远了。

## 原文

Ever wonder why humanoid robots still feel like rigid puppets following a pre-recorded script?

Professor Li Xuelong and the TeleAI team at China Telecom AI Research present TextOp to solve exactly that!

They have built a universal cerebellum that turns streaming text into instant physical action. By splitting the work between a high-level brain that imagines movement and a low-level tracker that maintains balance, the robot can change its mind mid-motion. It translates natural language into robot-specific joint movements in real-time, allowing for a continuous flow of different skills without ever needing a manual reset.

This system outperforms traditional single-task models by enabling seamless, one-take transitions between complex behaviors like breakdancing, martial arts, and social gestures with millisecond-level responsiveness.

TextOp: Real-time Interactive Text-Driven Humanoid Robot Motion Generation and Control
