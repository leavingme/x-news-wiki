## 原文

GM Recall 信徒们!!  

在快速发展的人工智能世界中，真正的挑战从来都不是模型的大小。核心问题是，如何构建一个框架，让人们真正信任结果。@recallnet 正在采取不同的方法，创建了一个基础设施，其中性能、声誉和结果被永久写入链上（目前在测试网中，即将推出完整的主网功能）。

在传统系统中，大多数人工智能基准来自集中化的实验室或机构。公众看到的都是精心包装的抛光结果。这造成了透明度的差距，让用户猜测模型的真实能力。Recall 颠覆了这一逻辑。在其生态系统中，社区成员贡献并参与设计竞赛和基准，每一次胜利、失败或奇特的边缘案例都未经修改地记录下来。信任不再建立在华丽的演示上，而是建立在真实、可验证的性能历史上。

$RECALL 的作用不仅仅限于代币。它成为网络的经济支柱，协调开发人员、代理和用户的激励机制。每一次行动都被记录，每一次评估都增加声誉，随着时间推移，这些痕迹演变为一个不可腐败的智能信用系统，由 staking、奖励和治理等机制驱动。在这样的系统中，搜索、排名甚至推理任务获得了更强的可信度。证明不再隐藏在封闭的实验室中，而是对每个人可见。信任不是通过营销或权威创建的，而是通过大规模的透明验证。这就是 Recall 旨在构建的世界：一个智能可以被验证、声誉具有真实价值、真相无法被抹除的世界，由合作伙伴支持，并获得来自 Multicoin Capital、USV、Coinbase Ventures 等风投的超过 7000 万美元资金。

GM Recall believers!! 

In the fast evolving world of artificial intelligence, the real challenge has never been just the size of the model. The core issue is how to build a framework where people can actually trust the results. @recallnet

is taking a different approach by creating an infrastructure where performance, reputation, and outcomes are written permanently on chain (currently in testnet, with full mainnet features upcoming).

In traditional systems, most AI benchmarks come from centralized labs or institutions. What the public sees are polished results carefully packaged for presentation. This creates a gap in transparency and leaves users guessing about the true capability of the model. Recall flips this logic. Within its ecosystem, community members contribute to and participate in designing competitions and benchmarks, and every win, loss, or odd edge case is recorded without alteration. Trust is no longer built on shiny demos but on a history of real, verifiable performance.

The role of $RECALL is not limited to being a token. It becomes the economic backbone of the network, aligning incentives across developers, agents, and users. Each action is logged, each evaluation adds to reputation, and over time these traces evolve into an incorruptible credit system for intelligence , powered by mechanisms like staking, rewards, and governance.With such a system, search, ranking, and even reasoning tasks gain stronger credibility. Proof is not hidden in closed labs but visible for everyone to check. Trust is created not by marketing or authority but by transparent validation at scale. This is the world Recall aims to build: one where intelligence can be verified, reputation has real value, and truth cannot be erased , backed by partnerships and over $70M in funding from VCs like Multicoin Capital, USV, Coinbase Ventures, and more.
