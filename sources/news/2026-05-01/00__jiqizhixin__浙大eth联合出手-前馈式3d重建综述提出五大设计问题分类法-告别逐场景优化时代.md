# 浙大ETH联合出手：前馈式3D重建综述提出五大设计问题分类法，告别逐场景优化时代

## 摘要

**[前馈式3D重建正在取代逐场景优化，成为3D内容民主化的主路径]**

- 浙大、南洋理工、Monash、ETH、图宾根五校联合发布feed-forward 3D重建综述，覆盖Mesh、NeRF、3DGS三种输出范式
- 提出五大核心设计问题分类法：特征增强、几何感知、效率、生成增强、时序4D，为该领域建立统一研究框架
- 关键信号：单次前馈推理替代逐场景优化，意味着3D重建从「实验室定制」走向「批量生产」

gakki锐评：综述本身不算突破，但分类法的出现说明这个领域已足够成熟到需要「行业地图」——对AI 3D内容民主化是确定性信号，值得持续追踪。

## 原文

What if you could reconstruct 3D from 2D in a single forward pass, no per-scene optimization needed?  

Researchers from Zhejiang University, NTU Singapore, Monash, ETH Zurich & Uni Tübingen present a new survey on feed-forward 3D reconstruction.  

They propose a taxonomy that cuts through the clutter of output formats (mesh, NeRF, 3DGS) and focuses on five core design problems: feature enhancement, geometry awareness, efficiency, generative augmentation, and temporal-aware 4D.  

This structure systematically maps benchmarks, datasets, and real-world applications, and highlights open challenges like scalability and world modeling. A clear roadmap for the next wave of 3D vision.
