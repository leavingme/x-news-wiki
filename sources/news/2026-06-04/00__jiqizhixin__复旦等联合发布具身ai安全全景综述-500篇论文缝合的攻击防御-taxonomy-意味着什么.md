# 复旦等联合发布具身AI安全全景综述：500篇论文缝合的攻击防御 taxonomy 意味着什么

## 摘要

**【一句话核心判断】**
复旦大学、香港城市大学、UIUC等联合发布首个具身AI安全综合综述，将感知、认知、规划、动作、人机交互全链路的攻击防御统一为一套taxonomy，填补了该领域长期碎片化的空白。

**【信息增量】**
1. 首次跨感知-认知-动作全链路梳理安全威胁，而非单一模块；
2. 对抗patch到jailbreak prompt，覆盖面超500篇相关文献；
3. 具身AI安全从「单点防御」进入「系统框架」阶段。

**【影响】**
对机器人/自动驾驶/医疗辅助等具身AI部署方，以及安全研究者，这是一个基础性参考文献的诞生。后续安全基准大概率会以这篇综述的框架为蓝本。


**gakki锐评：** 这篇综述的真正价值不是新发现，而是「把散落的东西拼起来让人看清楚全貌」——具身AI安全的系统性风险正在超过单点模型安全的重要性，因为物理世界的伤害是真实可感的。

## 原文

What happens when your robot car or healthcare assistant makes a dangerous mistake?

Researchers from Fudan University, City University of Hong Kong, UIUC, and others present the first comprehensive survey on embodied AI safety. 

They map out every class of attack and defense across perception, cognition, planning, action, and human-robot interaction — from adversarial patches to jailbreak prompts. 

The survey reviews over 500 papers and outperforms prior works by unifying fragmented research. It reveals critical overlooked challenges: fragile multimodal sensor fusion, planning instability under attacks, and trustworthiness in open-ended human-agent interaction. A roadmap for building safe, robust embodied agents.

Safety in Embodied AI: A Survey of Risks, Attacks, and Defenses
