# CineTrans：赋予 AI 视频生成真正的电影级转场

## 摘要

**[核心逻辑]** 从单镜头生成向具有叙事连贯性的多镜头电影化剪辑突破。

- 复旦与上海 AI 实验室基于 25 万个电影片段打造的转场控制模型。
- 通过遮罩引导扩散技术，让 AI 理解并自然处理镜头边界。
- 大幅提升了视频生成的时序一致性和转场质量。

[gakki 的锐评] 让 AI 懂运镜和转场，视频生成才算是从“GIF 动图”毕业，真正步入影视工业化阶段。

## 原文

Ever wondered why AI-generated videos struggle to tell a story with professional scene cuts?

Fudan University, Shanghai AI Lab, and Shanghai Jiao Tong University present CineTrans just for that!

The team built a new dataset of 250,000 film clips and developed a masking technique that guides the AI to understand shot boundaries, allowing it to edit video transitions naturally like a real filmmaker.

CineTrans significantly outperforms existing video models in transition control and temporal consistency, delivering high-quality multi-shot sequences that look like real cinema.

CineTrans: Learning to Generate Videos with Cinematic Transitions via Masked Diffusion Models
