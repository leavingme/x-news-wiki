# ReBalance：哈工深+华为+清华用实时置信度驯服推理冗余，训练-free 的 plug-and-play 方案覆盖 0.5B 到 32B

## 摘要

**推理过程的「恰到好处」终于有了工程化解法。**

- 核心机制：实时检测模型是「想多了」还是「想浅了」，动态引导剪枝或深入探索，无需重训练
- 在数学、QA、代码三大类共 9 个 benchmark 上，从 0.5B 到 32B 模型均实现冗余压缩 + 准确率提升的双赢
- 训练-free + plug-and-play 意味着今天就能接入现有推理流水线

gakki 锐评：推理效率优化正在从「压缩 token 数」的粗放阶段进入「什么时候该深想、什么时候该快答」的细粒度调度——ReBalance 的价值不在于模型更强，而在于同样的模型花更少的废话做对更多的题。

## 原文

What if your AI could stop overthinking simple problems while still exploring tough ones? 

Researchers from HIT Shenzhen, Huawei, Tsinghua, and others present ReBalance. 

It uses real-time confidence to detect overthinking or underthinking, then gently steers the model’s reasoning to prune waste or encourage deeper exploration. 

Across nine math, QA, and coding benchmarks on models from 0.5B to 32B, it cuts output redundancy while boosting accuracy. A training-free, plug-and-play upgrade for smarter, balanced reasoning. Details: https://t.co/pSPb5iztZR.
