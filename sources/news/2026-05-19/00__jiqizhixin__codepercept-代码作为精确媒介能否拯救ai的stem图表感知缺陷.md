# CodePercept：代码作为精确媒介能否拯救AI的STEM图表感知缺陷

## 摘要

**上海交大与Qwen团队提出CodePercept，聚焦"感知 scaling"而非"推理 scaling"。**

核心逻辑：用代码生成图像-标注-代码三元组，消除视觉幻觉与歧义，将感知精度问题转化为可验证的代码执行问题。

结果：在STEM视觉感知任务上超越推理优先方案，设定新的可验证理解基准。

这说明了一个方向性问题：纯靠CoT scaling解决不了视觉感知错误，感知层的结构化约束可能比推理链更根本。

## 原文

Why do AI models fail at STEM diagrams? Is it weak reasoning or poor perception?

Researchers from Shanghai Jiao Tong University and Alibaba’s Qwen Team present CodePercept.

Instead of scaling reasoning, they scale perception using code as a precise medium—generating image-caption-code triplets to eliminate hallucinations and ambiguity.

Result: Outperform reasoning-focused approaches in STEM visual perception, setting a new benchmark for verifiable comprehension.
