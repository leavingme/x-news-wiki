# LIFE 框架把多 Agent 自进化从"模块堆叠"升级为"因果闭环"：国内学术+工业联合范式的标杆

## 摘要

**[多 Agent 研究从"各自堆模块"开始收口为"自进化因果链路"]**

- LIFE 四阶段（构建能力→团队整合→归因找错→自进化）首次把 capability / integration / attribution / evolution 因果串联，超越过往孤立综述
- 西安交大+联想+悉尼大学三方联合，国内"学术+工业"合作产出统一框架的典型范式
- 瞄准 self-organizing + resilient multi-agent 系统，把工程经验沉淀为可复现研究对象


gakki 锐评：统一框架的价值在于让"经验性调优"变成"可证伪研究"——但 self-evolution 在真实部署里能不能跑通，归因环节的 benchmark 硬度才是真正的试金石。

## 原文

What if AI agents could not only collaborate but also learn from their own failures and evolve? 

Researchers from Xi'an Jiaotong University, Lenovo, and the University of Sydney present a new survey. 

They introduce the LIFE progression: build agent capabilities, integrate them in teams, find faults through attribution, and enable self-evolution. 

This unified framework outperforms prior isolated surveys by causally linking these stages, paving the way for self-organizing, resilient multi-agent intelligence.

Beyond Individual Intelligence: Surveying Collaboration, Failure Attribution, and Self-Evolution in LLM-based Multi-Agent Systems
