# EvalVerse：让AI视频评价脱离"像素级"，进入电影工业标准

## 摘要

**[评测范式升级：从Prompt遵循到美学判断]**

港科大×腾讯×斯坦福团队推出EvalVerse，用专家校准的视觉-语言模型评判AI视频的 cinematography quality、acting、aesthetics，超越仅看"是否符合指令"的传统Benchmark。

• 覆盖多镜头序列和音视频整合，填补复杂视频评估空白
• 将电影工业的专业标准引入AI评测，消除"肉眼主观"的争议
• benchmark本身成为新的基础设施，影响模型迭代方向

gakki：一旦"好看"可以被量化，AI视频的军备竞赛就从"能生成"升级到"有审美"——这个转变会杀死一大批"能跑就行"的短视频生成工具。

## 原文

Can AI-generated video ever match professional cinema? 

A team from HKUST, Tencent, and Stanford introduces EvalVerse. 

It treats video evaluation as a science—using expert-calibrated vision-language models to judge not just if the output follows the prompt, but also its cinematic quality, acting, and aesthetics. 

Outperforms prior benchmarks by expanding criteria to “goodness” and covering complex multi-shot sequencing and audio-visual integration.
