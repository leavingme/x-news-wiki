# 人大+字节Seed发布Agent-World：多环境强化学习让Agent自我进化训练

## 摘要

**Agent训练范式从静态数据集转向真实工具生态的自演化竞技场。**

- Agent-World自动发现真实世界工具生态（API、数据库等），合成可验证、难度可调的任务
- 采用多环境强化学习，持续识别并修补Agent能力缺口
- 在23个高难度benchmark上完成验证，覆盖工具调用、多步推理等核心能力

这是Agent基建层的重要信号：训练数据不再是瓶颈，环境生成才是。字节Seed与人大的组合也暗示国产Agent研究正在从「跟跑」转向「定义赛道」。

## 原文

What if your AI agent could train itself in thousands of real-world environments instead of static datasets?

Renmin University of China and ByteDance Seed introduce Agent-World, a self-evolving training arena. 

It automatically discovers real-world tool ecosystems (like APIs and databases) and synthesizes verifiable tasks with adjustable difficulty, then uses multi-environment reinforcement learning to continuously identify and fix capability gaps.

Across 23 challenging benchmarks, Agent-World models (8B and 14B parameters) consistently beat strong proprietary models and environment scaling baselines. More environments and self-evolution rounds = better intelligence.
