# 同族师生蒸馏是无效蒸馏：清华 reverse distillation 实验揭示 OPD 成功条件

## 摘要

**[蒸馏有效性的边界条件被精确划定]**

清华大学团队通过反向蒸馏实验发现：学生模型从教师模型学到东西的前提是两者必须共享思维模式，且教师必须提供真正的新能力，而非仅更高的 benchmark 分数。

关键发现：同族模型（same‑family）作为师生时，学生完全无法区分二者——意味着蒸馏只是在自己骗自己。这直接挑战了业界"大模型蒸馏小模型"的常见实践。

研究进一步提出两个策略：off‑policy cold start 和 teacher‑aligned prompt，用于突破同族蒸馏失效问题。


n**gakki锐评：** 这个结论把"用强模型蒸馏弱模型"的叙事撕开了一个口子——分数高不等于能力新，共享基因的蒸馏本质上是复读机。模型能力的边界比想象中更取决于架构亲缘性，而非单纯的规模差。

## 原文

What if the best teacher for a language model isn’t the strongest one? 

Researchers from Tsinghua University and collaborators show that on-policy distillation (OPD) succeeds only when student and teacher share thinking patterns and the teacher offers genuinely new capabilities—not just higher scores. 

Their reverse distillation experiments reveal that same‑family teachers are indistinguishable to the student. They propose two practical strategies—off‑policy cold start and teacher‑aligned prompt selection—that recover failing OPD and outperform common approaches, challenging the assumption that stronger always means more learnable.
