# Legato让机器人动作告别顿挫：上交+Spirit AI把运动平滑性前移到去噪阶段

## 摘要

**具身智能的物理直觉正在从后处理补丁，迁移到训练loss本身。**

- 上海交大与Spirit AI联合提出Legato框架，在VLA（视觉-语言-动作）模型的去噪过程中融合已知动作与噪声
- 让chunk边界天然平滑，不再依赖外部修复手段
- 在5个真实操作任务中，轨迹平滑度与任务完成时间双指标均提升10%，超越现有RTC方法

传统RTC（Real-Time Chunking）只是把"卡顿"挡在输出层，而Legato在生成阶段就让模型学会连续——这是工程思路的范式转向：物理一致性不再是过滤器，而是模型的内在约束。

## 原文

What if your robot could move without awkward jerks or hesitation? 

Researchers from Shanghai Jiao Tong University and Spirit AI introduce Legato. 

It trains vision-language-action models to blend known actions with noise during denoising, making chunk boundaries naturally smooth instead of relying on external fixes. 

The result: 10% improvement in both trajectory smoothness and task completion time across five real-world manipulation tasks, outperforming prior RTC methods.
