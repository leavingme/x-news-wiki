## 原文

What if you could design a new nanobody from scratch with only tens of lab tests instead of millions?

MoleculeMind (led by Jinbo Xu) presents MMDesign – a platform that generates and filters nanobody candidates from just a target protein and desired binding site.

The system uses AI to optimize both the structure and sequence of nanobodies, then applies multiple filters for reliability, naturalness, and binding quality – shrinking the pool from thousands to just 14–50 testable designs per target.

Tested on 11 therapeutically relevant targets (cytokines, immune checkpoints, receptors, viral proteins), MMDesign achieved confirmed binders for 10 out of 11 (90.9%) with picomolar to nanomolar affinities – including a 50% hit rate on notoriously difficult TNFα. This outperforms traditional discovery by requiring far fewer experimental candidates.

Practical De Novo Nanobody Discovery with Tens of Experimental Candidates
