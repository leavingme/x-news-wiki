# CHAI：卡内基梅隆×哈佛用专业 filmmaker 视觉基元重新定义视频字幕，Gemini 3.1 Pro 被超越

## 摘要

**CHAI 用结构化视频语言重新定义 AI 视频理解。**

数百个由专业 filmmaker 定义的视觉基元（visual primitives）构建了底层描述体系，而非让模型自由发挥。

训练流程引入「专家批判-模型迭代」闭环：专业字幕员反复校正 AI 输出，模型从中学习微妙的叙事语法。

结果：击败 Gemini 3.1 Pro，且能让 Wan 等视频生成模型遵循 400 词 prompt 指令——意味着字幕理解已开始反向驱动生成质量。

## 原文

What if AI could caption videos with the precision of a professional filmmaker?

Researchers from Carnegie Mellon University and Harvard University introduce CHAI just for that.

They built a structured video language using hundreds of visual primitives defined with filmmakers. Then, trained experts critique and refine AI-generated captions, boosting accuracy and efficiency.

The resulting model beats Gemini 3.1 Pro and even fine-tunes video generators like Wan to follow 400-word prompts, unlocking fine-grained control over camera motion, angle, and framing.

Building a Precise Video Language with Human-AI Oversight
