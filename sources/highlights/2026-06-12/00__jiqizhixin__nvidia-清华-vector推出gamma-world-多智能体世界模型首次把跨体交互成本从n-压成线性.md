# NVIDIA×清华×Vector推出Gamma-World：多智能体世界模型首次把跨体交互成本从N²压成线性

## 摘要

**多智能体世界建模的算力瓶颈被撕开一道口子。**

核心创新两点：
- Simplex编码：把每个Agent当作独特相位处理，告别固定顺序依赖
- Sparse Hub Attention：跨Agent计算从O(N²)降到线性，100个Agent的世界不再是算力禁区

效果：在视频质量上同时击败slot-based和dense-attention方案。

价值：这不是又一个World Model demo，而是把"多人/多机器人/多AI共存的游戏世界"从论文推到可工程化的临界点。对具身智能仿真、群体机器人训练、开放世界AI NPC都是基础设施级利好。

gakki锐评：当单Agent的World Model已经卷到审美疲劳，多智能体扩展性才是真正卡死应用的瓶颈。清华+Vector+NVIDIA的组合意味着明年游戏引擎、机器人仿真平台都会跟进这条路——单纯堆参数的研究范式该收一收了。

## 原文

What if your video game world could handle multiple agents—players, robots, or AIs—without breaking a sweat?

NVIDIA, @Tsinghua_Uni  , and Vector Institute introduce Gamma-World, a generative multi-agent world model. 

They invented two tricks: a "simplex" encoding that treats each agent as a unique phase (no fixed ordering), and "sparse hub attention" that slashes cross-agent computing from quadratic to linear cost.

Result: Gamma-World beats slot-based and dense-attention methods in video quality, action control, and agent consistency—and scales effortlessly from 2 to 4 players, all at 24 FPS.
