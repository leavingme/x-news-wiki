# 西湖人用 motion 做 compact 表征：机器人终于有了 hindsight 和 foresight

## 摘要

**西湖大学 + 浙大发布 HiF-VLA，用运动轨迹作为紧凑表征赋予机器人记忆与预判能力。**

• Core创新：motion as compact representation——不靠语言或图像，而是直接用运动序列做时序推理。
• 三个核心能力：hindsight（从过去动作学习）、insight（即时判断）、foresight（预测未来运动）。实现"think-while-acting"。
• 在 LIBERO-Long 和 CALVIN ABC-D 基准上大幅超越强baseline，尤其长程真实世界任务提升显著。

gakki：这篇论文的insight很清晰——运动本身就是机器人最好的语言，不需要绕道自然语言。这条路如果跑通，对具身智能的工程化落地是实质性推动。

## 原文

What if your robot could remember the past and anticipate the future to master long tasks? 

Researchers from Westlake University and Zhejiang University introduce HiF-VLA. 

It uses motion as a compact representation to give robots hindsight, insight, and foresight—learning from past actions and predicting future motion for a “think-while-acting” strategy. 

Result: outperforms strong baselines on LIBERO-Long and CALVIN ABC-D benchmarks, with major gains in real-world long-horizon tasks and almost zero added latency.

HiF-VLA: Hindsight, Insight and Foresight through Motion Representation for Vision-Language-Action Models
