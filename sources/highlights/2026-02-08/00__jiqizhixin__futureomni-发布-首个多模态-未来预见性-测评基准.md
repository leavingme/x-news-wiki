# FutureOmni 发布：首个多模态“未来预见性”测评基准

## 摘要

**[复旦与新加坡国立大学联合推动 AI 从“描述现状”转向“预测未来”]**
- 挑战模型通过音视频线索预判接下来 1-5 秒内可能发生的物理事件
- 现有顶尖模型准确率瓶颈位于 65% 左右，显示因果推理能力的欠缺
- 提出全新的全模态训练策略，强化模型对时空连续性的认知

## 原文

What if AI could predict the future from sights and sounds? 

Researchers from Fudan University & NUS introduce FutureOmni, the first benchmark to test this. 

It pushes multimodal models to reason across audio & video to forecast what happens next, not just describe the past. 

Results show current models struggle, with top accuracy at just 64.8%. Their new training strategy, however, boosts performance on future prediction and general reasoning tasks.

FutureOmni: Evaluating Future Forecasting from Omni-Modal Context for Multimodal LLMs
