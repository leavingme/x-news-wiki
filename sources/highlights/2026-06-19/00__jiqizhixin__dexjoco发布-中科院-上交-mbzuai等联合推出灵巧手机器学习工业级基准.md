# DexJoCo发布：中科院/上交/MBZUAI等联合推出灵巧手机器学习工业级基准

## 摘要

灵巧手benchmark从单任务演示走向「任务导向+域随机化」的工业级评测阶段。
中科院、上交、MBZUAI、北大、港中文联合推出DexJoCo，11个以人为中心的任务，1.1K演示轨迹，配套低成本数据采集系统。
内置域随机化用于鲁棒性测试，结果显示任务多样性与评估深度均优于现有灵巧手benchmark。

锐评：灵巧手评测长期缺乏统一标准，「每个实验室自建任务集」的混乱阻碍了横向比较；DexJoCo用任务多样性+域随机化补了这个缺口，下一步看主流具身实验室是否真把它当基准用。

## 原文

Can robotic hands truly handle complex tasks like tool use and bimanual coordination? 

CASIA, SJTU, MBZUAI, PKU, and CUHK introduce DexJoCo — a new benchmark and toolkit built for task-oriented dexterous manipulation. 

It features 11 human-grounded tasks, a low-cost data collection system, and 1.1K demonstration trajectories with built-in domain randomization for robustness testing. 

Results show DexJoCo outperforms existing dexterous benchmarks in task diversity and evaluation depth, while revealing key limitations in current AI policies — setting fresh challenges for the next generation of robotic hands.
