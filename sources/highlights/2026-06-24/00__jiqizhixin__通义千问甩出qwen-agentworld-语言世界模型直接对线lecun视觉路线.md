# 通义千问甩出Qwen-AgentWorld：语言世界模型直接对线LeCun视觉路线

## 摘要

**阿里通义千问发布首个纯语言世界模型Qwen-AgentWorld，证明世界动态不一定要靠视觉表征来预测。**

- 35B/397B双规格模型，在7个领域（工具/终端/OS任务等）的10M+真实交互轨迹上训练
- 三阶段训练流水线：通用世界建模→下一状态推理→强化学习保真度
- 核心突破：用长CoT推理直接预测环境动态，开辟语言世界模型新范式
- 与LeCun V-JEPA视觉世界模型形成路线分叉


gakki锐评：语言世界模型不是LeCun JEPA的复制品，是用Transformer推理能力替代视觉表征的另一种赌注——如果这条路走通，机器人/Agent领域对视觉数据的依赖将被结构性削弱。

## 原文

What if an AI could simulate any digital environment just by thinking in language?  

Qwen Team presents Qwen-AgentWorld — the first language world models that predict environment dynamics via long chain-of-thought reasoning.  

They trained two models (35B and 397B) on 10M+ real-world interaction trajectories across 7 domains (tools, terminals, OS tasks, etc.) using a three-stage pipeline: general world modeling, next-state reasoning, and reinforcement learning for simulation fidelity.  

Result: Qwen-AgentWorld significantly outperforms all frontier models on AgentWorldBench, a new benchmark covering 5 dimensions of simulation quality. As a decoupled simulator, it also boosts agent RL beyond real-environment training, and as a warm-up for unified agents, it improves performance across 7 agentic benchmarks.

Qwen-AgentWorld: Language World Models for General Agents
