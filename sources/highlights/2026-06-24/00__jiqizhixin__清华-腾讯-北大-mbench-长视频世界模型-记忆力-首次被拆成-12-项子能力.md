# 清华×腾讯×北大 MBench：长视频世界模型'记忆力'首次被拆成 12 项子能力

## 摘要

**MBench 重新定义视频世界模型评测——从'看着像'升级到'记得住'。**

- 清华、腾讯、北大联合提出，针对长视频世界模型的长程记忆能力
- 评测维度分 12 个子技能，覆盖物体识别、环境延续、因果链条
- 评测方法采用真实长视频 + 自动化规则 + 视觉语言模型混合打分


gakki 锐评：世界模型赛道正在从'生成质量内卷'转向'认知能力外卷'，MBench 撕开了新战场。

## 原文

Can video world models truly remember what they see over time?

Researchers from Tsinghua University, Tencent, and Peking University present MBench, a new benchmark that tests memory in video AI. 

Instead of just checking if videos look good, MBench measures whether models keep track of objects, environments, and cause-and-effect across long video sequences.

Using real long videos and a mix of automated rules and vision-language AI, MBench breaks memory down into 12 specific sub-skills. Testing today’s top video world models shows they still fail badly at retaining consistent state over time, revealing a critical gap for building true world models.

MBench: A Comprehensive Benchmark on Memory Capability for Video World Models
