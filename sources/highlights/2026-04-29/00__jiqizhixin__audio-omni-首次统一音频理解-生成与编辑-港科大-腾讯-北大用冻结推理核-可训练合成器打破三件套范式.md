# Audio-Omni 首次统一音频理解、生成与编辑：港科大+腾讯+北大用冻结推理核+可训练合成器打破三件套范式

## 摘要

**一个模型同时理解、生成和编辑所有类型音频——这是音频多模态的「GPT-4 时刻」。**

- 港科大、腾讯、北大联合提出 Audio-Omni 端到端框架，覆盖通用声音、音乐、语音三大类
- 架构核心：冻结的 Multimodal LLM 作为推理大脑 + 可训练的 Diffusion Transformer 作为合成器
- 在多个 benchmark 上达到 SOTA，首次证明音频任务可以在单一模型内闭环
- 意味着 TTS、音乐生成、音频编辑不再需要三条独立流水线

**gakki 锐评：** 音频多模态一直是被视觉多模态遮蔽的战场，Audio-Omni 的「冻结推理核」架构思路和视觉领域的 Frozen BiLM 一脉相承——先锁住理解能力，再轻量化生成端，这条路径正在成为跨模态统一的工程共识。

## 原文

What if your AI could not only understand sound but also generate and edit it, all in one model?

Researchers from HKUST, Tencent, and Peking University introduce Audio-Omni.

It’s the first end-to-end framework that unifies audio understanding, generation, and editing across general sound, music, and speech. Think of it as a frozen reasoning brain (Multimodal LLM) paired with a trainable synthesizer (Diffusion Transformer).

The result? State-of-the-art performance across benchmarks, matching or beating specialized expert models. It also gains emergent abilities like knowledge-augmented reasoning generation and zero-shot cross-lingual control. Code, model, and a new 1M+ editing dataset are coming soon.
