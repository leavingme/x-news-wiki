# Reagent：不是给答案打分，而是给每一步推理「挑毛病」

## 摘要

**实时推理纠错的粒度革命：从终点评价到过程解剖。**

- CUHK 与美团团队提出 Reagent，对每个推理步骤提供详细批判
- 识别具体逻辑缺陷，给 Agent 结构化路线图实时修正思维
- 12 个基准测试性能大幅提升，证明「过程监督」优于「结果监督」

Agent 训练的下一个前沿：不是奖励正确答案，而是惩罚错误思考路径。

## 原文

Ever wonder why AI agents struggle with complex tasks even when they get the final answer right?

Researchers from CUHK and Meituan present Reagent just for that!

Instead of just giving a thumbs up or down at the end of a task, this new method provides a detailed critique of every reasoning step. It identifies specific logical flaws and gives agents a structured roadmap to refine their thinking in real-time.

This approach delivers massive performance leaps across 12 different benchmarks, achieving 43.7 percent on GAIA and 46.2 percent on WebWalkerQA, proving that teaching agents how to think is more effective than just checking their work.
