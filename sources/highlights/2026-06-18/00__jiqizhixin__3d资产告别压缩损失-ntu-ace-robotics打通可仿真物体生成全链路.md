# 3D资产告别压缩损失：NTU+ACE Robotics打通可仿真物体生成全链路

## 摘要

一句话核心判断：南洋理工×ACE Robotics 把刚体/可形变/铰接三类可仿真3D资产统一一套系统，VL模型直读，几何不再因压缩掉精度。

要点：
• 新几何表示避免传统压缩损失，视觉-语言模型可端到端消化
• 六维属性（几何/尺度/材质/可供性/运动学/描述）全面超越现有方法
• 既是生成也是理解——同一系统双向打通，对机器人/具身训练数据链路意义大


Gakki 锐评：从"看起来像"跨到"能直接进仿真器"的临界点，具身AI/机器人最缺的可交互3D数据终于有了流水线；但 Sim2Real gap 仍是真正的产品生死线——能仿不等于能用。

## 原文

What if creating 3D assets for simulation was as simple as describing them?

NTU and ACE Robotics present PhysX-Omni: a unified system that generates simulation-ready rigid, deformable, and articulated objects. It uses a new efficient geometry representation for vision-language models, avoiding compression loss.

The result? Outperforms existing methods in both generation and understanding across six key attributes—geometry, scale, material, affordance, kinematics, and description. Plus, it unlocks realistic scene creation and robotics policy learning. This is a leap for embodied AI and physics-based simulation.
