# 具身智能闯入科研深水区：AutoBio 构建高保真生物实验室数字孪生，为机器人科学家铺路

## 摘要

**[具身智能正从家庭场景迈向复杂科研领域，数字孪生成为关键训练场]**
- 港大、清华、上交等团队推出 AutoBio 框架，构建高保真生物实验室数字孪生，模拟真实物理与透明器皿渲染。
- 该环境专为训练机器人执行复杂生物实验协议设计，标志着具身智能应用场景的重大拓展。
- 这不仅是技术突破，更是AI赋能基础科研范式的潜在拐点，机器人科学家或将从仿真走向现实。

当机器人开始在虚拟实验室里“搬砖”搞科研，我们离AI驱动的科学发现自动化又近了一步。这不仅是工具升级，更是对“谁来做科研”的根本性重构。

## 原文

Can generalist robots move beyond simple household chores and master the complex world of scientific research?

Researchers from HKU, Tsinghua University, Shanghai Jiaotong University, and TeleAI introduce AutoBio.

This framework creates a high-fidelity digital twin of a biology lab, using realistic physics for scientific tools and advanced rendering for transparent glassware. It provides a specialized training ground for robots to learn how to follow complex biological protocols through a vision-language-action pipeline.

Initial tests show that current top-tier AI models struggle with the extreme precision and visual reasoning needed for lab work, revealing a significant performance gap between domestic tasks and professional scientific workflows.

AutoBio: A Simulation and Benchmark for Robotic Automation in Digital Biology Laboratory
