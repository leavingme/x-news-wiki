# 清华等发布 WorldArena：世界模型不能只看“画质”，更要看“脑力”

## 摘要

**[核心逻辑]** 针对具身智能的新基准，测试世界模型在预测视频真实性的同时，是否真的具备辅助 AI 代理进行规划的功能性价值。

- **认知鸿沟**：研究发现高画质生成模型在功能任务（如规划）上表现极差，揭示了感知与逻辑之间的断层。
- **评价体系升级**：从单一的视觉感知向具身实用性评分转移，助力物理智能闭环。

[gakki 的锐评]：光会做梦（画图）没用，AI 必须学会在梦里预演如何走路。这就是“物理智能”的深水区。

## 原文

How do we know if an AI's "imagination" is actually useful for real-world tasks?

Researchers from institutions including Tsinghua University and Zhejiang Lab introduce WorldArena, a new unified benchmark. 

It tests AI world models not just on how realistic their predicted videos look, but on how well those predictions help an AI agent perform tasks.

The results show a major gap: models that generate high-quality videos often fail at functional tasks like planning. WorldArena outperforms fragmented, perception-only evaluations by providing a holistic score for embodied AI.

WorldArena: A Unified Benchmark for Evaluating Perception and Functional Utility of Embodied World Models
